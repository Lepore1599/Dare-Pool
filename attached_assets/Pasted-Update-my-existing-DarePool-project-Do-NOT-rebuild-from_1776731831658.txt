FINAL DARE STRUCTURE TO IMPLEMENT:

SUCCESSFUL DARE FLOW:
1. Dare is posted
2. 48-hour timer starts immediately
3. Users can fund the dare while it is live
4. Users can submit attempts while it is live
5. Users can vote on submissions while it is live
6. When the 48-hour timer ends:
   - submissions close
   - voting closes
   - the valid submission with the most votes wins
7. Payout split:
   - 80% to winning submission user

   - 10% to platform

FAILED DARE FLOW:
If the dare ends with NO VALID SUBMISSIONS:
- No winner is selected
- No payout split is taken
- 100% of the pool is transferred to another eligible active dare
- Users who funded the dead dare are notified where the money moved
